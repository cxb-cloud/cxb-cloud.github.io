Dorian Vale

Dorian Vale is a thinker, provocateur, and zine-maker obsessed with questions of identity, consciousness, and otherness. Through essays, illustrations, and experimental zines, Vale interrogates how humans define themselves in relation to the world, animals, and each other. In “Dorian Vale's 'Horse and also Man,'” Dorian challenges readers to reconsider ethical frameworks, blurring the line between species and exploring radical notions of freedom, empathy, and perception.

Experimental fiction and urban poetry exploring desire and obsession.

Hybrid fiction and poetry, often fragmented and diaristic. Claire’s work moves between prose, tech, and poetry, often blurring the lines of genre. She explores the inner lives of people in transit, both physically and emotionally. She collects found texts and uses them as prompts of new forms.

Max Wilder

Max Wilder is a wandering provocateur and self-styled architect of chaos. With a love for booze, campfires, and the absurd, Max’s work turns everyday life into a playground of rebellion. Anarchis-ti-sh philosophy meets hands-on mischief in his interactive guides, where readers are invited to subvert rules, challenge authority, and participate in unpredictable adventures. Wilder’s world is one where exploration, laughter, and calculated disorder are not just tolerated—they’re celebrated.

Lily Hart

Lily Hart is a contemporary poet known for her heartfelt and evocative verse. With her poetry collections Lily Hart explores themes of love, personal growth, and emotional resilience. Her writing resonates with readers worldwide, offering moments of introspection and emotional clarity.

Riley Storm

Riley Storm is an alchemist of words, weaving short fiction and poetry that probe love, sex, and existential reflection. Fascinated by the possibilities of language itself, Storm experiments with reordering text, fragmented forms, and unexpected syntax, creating work that surprises, seduces, and challenges. Their stories and poems are meditative explorations of human intimacy, pleasure, and the emotional currents that bind people together in contemporary life.

Coming Book:


Lesmmnera “Lesmmnera” is a collection of short stories and poems that examine love, sexuality, and existence with unflinching honesty. Through linguistic experiments and inventive narrative structures, Riley Storm offers a meditative, sometimes erotic, journey into the human condition.
